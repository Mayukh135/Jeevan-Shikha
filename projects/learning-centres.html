Overview

About This Programme

We operate 3 learning centres in the remote Van Gujjar communities of Thanda, Natthu Chhod, and Gujjar Basti in Uttarakhand. Each centre has a dedicated teacher who provides primary education, dividing children into groups based on their learning levels.

Many of these children cannot attend formal school due to extreme geographic remoteness or lack of community awareness. Beyond academics, children engage in games, sports, nature education, and arts. After completing primary education here, older children are helped to enroll in formal schools.

What We Do

Key Activities

Primary Education

Structured learning with child-paced grouping across literacy, numeracy, and general knowledge.

Art & Craft

Creative expression through drawing, painting, and handicrafts that connect to local culture.

Sports & Play

Physical activity and structured play that build confidence and community among children.

School Enrollment

Older children are supported in transitioning to formal schools after completing primary level.
