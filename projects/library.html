Community Library Programme

Assam BookWorm (Goa), Free Library Network
Overview

About This Programme

The Community Library programme is supported by BookWorm (Goa) and the Free Library Network. Field coordinators receive training to conduct structured reading and storytelling sessions for children and youth.

The programme provides books to community students and promotes learning through story-based messages that are culturally relevant and engaging. It helps nurture a reading habit in communities where books are rarely accessible.

Programme in Action

What We Do

Key Activities

Reading Sessions

Structured reading sessions led by trained field coordinators for children of different ages.

Storytelling

Interactive storytelling that builds language skills and encourages imagination in children.

Book Distribution

Books provided to community children to take home, building personal libraries for families.

Coordinator Training

Regular training for field coordinators to improve session quality and content delivery.
